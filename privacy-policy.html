Introduction

Thank you for using Math Buddy Kids. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app. Math Buddy Kids is a children's educational math app designed for offline use, and we are committed to protecting the privacy of all users, especially children.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, use, or share any personal information.

Math Buddy Kids is designed to work entirely offline. The app does not require account creation, login, or any form of registration. No personal data is transmitted from your device.

Math Buddy Kids does not use any third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking services. The app contains no ads and does not share data with any third parties.

Children's Privacy

Math Buddy Kids is intended for use by young children and early learners. We take children's privacy very seriously and comply with applicable child privacy laws, including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users. Since the app does not collect any data, there is no personal information to disclose, sell, or share.

In-App Purchases

Math Buddy Kids does not offer in-app purchases of any kind.

User-Generated Content

The app does not include chat features, social features, or any form of user-generated content.

Data Security

Because Math Buddy Kids does not collect or transmit any personal data, there is no risk of your personal information being accessed, altered, or disclosed through our app.
